b. `align-content` and `justify-content` - `align-content`: Aligns the whole grid's content along the block (vertical) axis. - `justify-content`: Aligns the whole grid's content along the inline (horizontal) axis. - Values: `start`, `end`, `center`, `space-between`, `space-around`, `space-evenly`, `stretch`.
